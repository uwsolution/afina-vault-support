Privacy Policy — Afina Vault

Effective date: 2026-02-26

Overview

Afina Vault is designed as a local, offline vault for storing sensitive keys and secrets on your device.

Data Collection

Afina Vault does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data to the developer or third parties.

Local Storage

Data you add is stored locally on your device in encrypted form. The app does not require account sign-in.

Camera Access

Camera permission is used only to scan QR codes when you choose to do so.

Network Usage

The app is intended for offline usage and does not rely on remote servers for core functionality.

Contact

Support: https://uwsolution.github.io/afina-vault-support/

If you have privacy questions, open an issue on the support page.
